About Me

I am a Master's student in Electronic Information at the University of Science and Technology of China, specializing in AI and medical image analysis. With experience as a research assistant and intern in AI technologies, my work focuses on deep learning applications in healthcare, including federated learning, disease detection, and privacy-preserving methods. I am passionate about leveraging machine learning to solve real-world problems in medical imaging and signal processing.
